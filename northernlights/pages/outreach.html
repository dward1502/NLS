Player Equipment Program

Northern Light Sports offers you the opportunity to give back to the world be donating used baseball and softball equipment. Each Box we send out has just what you need to play the game and more. 9 gloves, a set of bases, youth size bats, balls, paints, shirts.

We deliver the gear and creat a connection between player to player! For example if a team from a local USA Little League, Youth Team, High School donates its used equipment to a club or team internationally. We take a picture of them reciving the gear and then its game on!

What a way to make a deffeance to help build the grass roots development of the great game of baseball and softball.
