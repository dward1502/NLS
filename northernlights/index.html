Explore other nations while playing baseball.

See the sights when not playing in games

The mission of Northern Light Sports international is to provide opportunities for youth and young adults to develop leadership skills, healthy lifestyles, work ethic, commitment, honesty and integrity through sport and learning through other cultures. Northern Light Sports believes athletics teaches life lessons that can help people of all ages with success on and off the field. Northern Light Sports activities are also a platform to address challenging topics for each individual athlete. The goal for Northern Light Sports is to be a resource of support to help the Student Athletes and their families make educated decisions in the international and collegiate pursuit.

Fantastic Experience

"Traveling around Italy with Coach Dress was a fantastic experience. Coach was very amicable and fun to play baseball with. Not only did I learn a lot on my journey, but I got to enjoy the experience of a lifetime! Definitely an experience I will never forget and one that has made lasting changes on my life and in me as a ballplayer."
